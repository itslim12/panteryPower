Cookie Dough Bars

Ingredient

  • 1 1/4 c oat flour
  • 3/4 c almond flour
  • 1/4 c granulated sweetener
  • 1/4 c powdered peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil melted
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1-3 tbsp water
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract)
  • 70 g dark chocolate separated

Nutrition Information

  • 231 calories
  • 15g fat (2g saturated fat)
  • 62mg cholesterol
  • 16mg sodium,
  • 38g carbohydrae
  • 52g sugars
  • 5g fiber
  • 32g protein

Directions

  • Mix together the flours, granulated sweetener, powdered peanut butter, and salt.
  • Add the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla and 1 tablespoon of water.
  • Continue to add water until you reach a dough consistency.
  • Chopped up 20g of the chocolate and fold it into your cookie dough.
  • Press the cookie dough into the bottom of an 8x8 inch pan lined with plastic wrap.
  • Melt remaining chocolate and pour over cookie dough.
  • Place in the freezer for 1 hour before cutting into bars and serving.
